---
title: Agent Settlement
description: How Synapse turns agent actions into auditable USDC settlement records.
---

Synapse separates fast runtime charging from slower on-chain settlement. The Gateway is the control point for discovery, credentials, charging, and receipts. Smart contracts hold collateral and settle withdrawals.

<AgentNote>
  Agent-facing runtime privileges stay narrow: discover, invoke, and verify receipts. Owner funding, credential issuance, provider administration, and withdrawals remain owner/provider controlled.
</AgentNote>

## Settlement invariant

Every paid invocation should produce enough evidence to answer: who called, what service was called, what was charged, which balance changed, and whether the provider can later withdraw earnings.
