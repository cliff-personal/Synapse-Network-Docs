---
title: On-chain Withdrawal
description: How providers and owners turn off-chain balances into final USDC movement.
---

Withdrawal is intentionally slower and more controlled than invocation. Runtime calls are optimized for speed; withdrawals are optimized for correctness, auditability, and risk review.

## Settlement boundary

Gateway records receivables and prepares withdrawal evidence. Smart contracts remain the final boundary for USDC movement.

<AgentNote>
  Agents should not autonomously withdraw funds in V1. Treat withdrawal as an owner/provider operation requiring explicit wallet authority.
</AgentNote>
