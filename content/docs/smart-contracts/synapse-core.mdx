---
title: SynapseCore
description: The on-chain USDC collateral and settlement contract.
---

SynapseCore is deliberately boring. It should not contain service discovery, routing, pricing, budget velocity, provider health, or marketplace policy.

## Contract role

- Accept deposits.
- Hold USDC collateral.
- Support final withdrawal settlement.
- Preserve a clean audit boundary between Gateway receipts and chain movement.
